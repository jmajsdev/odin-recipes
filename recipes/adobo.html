Adobo Recipe

This is a Filipino style Pork adobo. I don't know what to say, it's a classic. It tastes good, well, depending on who cooks it. This dish is really easy to cook but at the same time, it's really easy to mess up. LOL.

Here are the ingredients for the basic Adobo (1kg. of meat)

Ingredients

  • Cooking Oil
  • Pork or Chicken
  • 4pcs Cloves of Garlic
  • Ginger only if the meat is chicken(idk why but it's the law!)
  • 1pc medium size Shallot or Onion
  • Salt
  • Peppers
  • 3-4pcs of Bay leaves
  • Soy Sauce to taste
  • Vinegar to taste
  • 3-4pcs Chilli Peppers(optional)

Steps

  1. Chop the pork/chicken into bite size cubes or however you want.
  2. (Optional) Marinade the meat with soy sauce, garlic and lime for 1-2hrs.
  3. Chop the garlic and onion/shallot into small pieces.
  4. On a heated pan with oil, saute garlic and onion until brownish.
  5. Put the meat and saute until brownish.
  6. Put the soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and peppers to taste.
  7. Wait for 15 - 20 minutes.
  8. Finally put the chilli peppers and bay leaves and leave it for another 5 minutes.
  9. Done. i mean, atleast thats how i cook it.
